# Commercial Support and Sustainability

This project is open source under the Apache License 2.0. You may use it for
personal, academic, internal, and commercial purposes according to that license.

Commercial sustainability is intended to come from services and ecosystem work
around the open-source project, not from restricting use of the core skill.

Possible commercial or sponsorship channels:

- custom thesis-defense deck workflows and template adaptation;
- enterprise support and maintenance;
- hosted visual QA or deck generation services;
- private template packs and institution-specific playbooks;
- training, documentation, and consulting;
- GitHub Sponsors or other voluntary sponsorship.

If you need commercial support, custom template adaptation, or enterprise
integration help, please contact the repository owner (`@zouchenzhen`) through
GitHub.
